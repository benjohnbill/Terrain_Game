# 06 — Carry a War to an Accepted Outcome

**What to build:** Let the player and bots repeat accepted atomic operations
until one war reaches a legal outcome selected for L3. The player can follow why
resistance ended and how the outcome changed forces, control, routes, and the
next strategic situation without a legacy shortcut or hidden forced closure.

**Blocked by:** 05 — Complete a Human–Bot Round.

Status: needs-info

Specification gates: Wayfinder 08, 10, and 12.

- [ ] The supported war-ending path is an accepted member of the capacity-or-will composite selected by the Production specification.
- [ ] Repeated operations remain atomic choices rather than an operation-in-progress stage conveyor.
- [ ] A human-controlled court is never force-closed by a stall, patience, or timeout policy.
- [ ] War-ending events explain the accepted capacity or will break and its territorial and force consequences.
- [ ] Settlement, capital, or field-army behavior not included in the L3 specification remains unavailable rather than approximated from legacy code.
- [ ] The complete war is reproducible from authored-world identity, seed, and ordered intent log.
