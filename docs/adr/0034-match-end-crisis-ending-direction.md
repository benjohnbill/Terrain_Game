## Context

The affordability-bound measurement (ADR 0033) left the frac-coupled wall
standing but showed the fgM9on reserve arm absorbs it. The grill promoted
the **FG board package WITH M9** (the fgM9on arm as measured, not an
M9-alone graft) and adopted **capLandFrac 1** — the build ceiling follows
conquered-population reality (nationalCap via usablePop: 60% start,
+10pp/turn ripen — already-live mechanics; those values stay HARNESS 가안;
intermediate fracs rejected as world-meaningless blends — the land-derived
identity argument). That world decides ~70% of matches; the remaining ~30%
reach turn 32 undecided. The question this ADR answers: what IS turn 32?

Clock ruling first: the envelope ruler (15–25 turn band, turn-32 bell) is
read on the **analyst track** — SPEC's 1.5–2 min/turn 가안 is KEPT; the
clicker track (~30s/turn) is always-shorter and always allowed; both
tracks' wall-clock calibration is an L3 debt (DESIGN-RISKS R12). On the
analyst track, turn 32 ≈ the one-hour mark — the bell is a real position,
not an arbitrary cap.

## Decision

The turn-32 ending is a **sudden-death CRISIS** (이민족 침입 family), not a
judged verdict:

- **Rule-driven** — no dice; deterministic (SPEC #4).
- **Preparable** — a hermit-kingdom strategy against it must be real.
- **Escalating encroachment** forcing an end within ~3 turns.
- The judged scorecard is **DEMOTED to last-resort fallback** (the
  pathological ~turn-35 case only). A plain judged verdict was REJECTED:
  endgame score-sitting is an identity violation (aggression ethos,
  DT-② / SPEC principles).
- Reframe: with the bell in place, decided% becomes a **KO-rate** — the
  ~30% non-KO matches are crisis finales, not failures of the ruler.

**Five design gates** bind the coming design pass:

1. Deterministic (no-dice) targeting.
2. Preparable = a real strategy (hermit kingdom must work).
3. Mechanical termination proof + the fallback.
4. Pressure distribution avoids BOTH leader-execution AND
   vulture-dominance (raid-archetype watch). Riders: (a) crown-holder
   structural advantage; (b) betrayal-politics layer — cooperation or
   defection vs the invasion is player choice; (c) 억까-legibility
   guardrail — betrayal must be readable (same constitution as risk R6 /
   skill-piercable).
5. Terrain resonance check — periphery rear-stab inverts 중원 exposure.

## Rejected alternatives

- Plain judged scorecard as the terminal (score-sitting identity
  violation; demoted to fallback, above).
- Reading the envelope on the clicker clock (would detach the bell from
  the SPEC's 30–40 min match promise; analyst track keeps it honest).
