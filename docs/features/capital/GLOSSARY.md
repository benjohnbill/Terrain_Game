# Capital — Glossary (Tier 1)

Birthplace definitions. Non-birthplace surfaces point here, never copy.

| Term | Definition | Status |
|---|---|---|
| capital (수도) | A political designation the player places on ONE of the seat's main city sectors at match start. The polity layer's map anchor: its fall is a regime event (collapse / forced-vassalage trigger), different in kind from an ordinary city's fall (value transfer). Distinct from 主 도시 (main city), which is a map fact (authored population/economy spike, battle-summoning); the capital is a status ON such a sector. History: RULINGS CP-①. | **AGREED-concept** (2026-07-10; wiring stage ②) |
| capital guard (근위대) | The capital's standing guard, land-derived: coefficient (가안 350) × capital sector populationValue. Garrison-class — place-bound, own local ceiling outside fieldCap, register-backed. One stock with the final-battle capital garrison: wear at the front thins the last stand. Forward capitals gain emergent value from it (the guard holds the gate, freeing the field army). History: RULINGS CP-① (incl. rejected flat-1500 reuse and ×1.25 hub buff). | **AGREED-concept** (2026-07-10; magnitude 가안, stage-② sweep) |
