# Strategy Space — Winning Archetypes (adopted 2026-07-04)

Derived backward from the hegemony inequality (GLOSSARY: 패권 결정점).
Role: **the dial checklist for every later magnitude/economy decision** —
before sealing any value, ask "which archetype does this kill?" (same
standing role as the combat-formula INDEX invariants).

## The four levers

Hegemony = leadership (my projectable mass ≥ ~1.7 × each in-balance
shield) ∧ unassailability (no coalition reaches ~1.7 × my shield within
the regeneration window). Only four things move it:

1. my projectable mass ↑
2. their shields ↓
3. my shield ↑ (raises the coalition's required mass)
4. the coalition pool ↓ (subtract via vassalage, erase via annihilation,
   or let rivals burn each other)

## Counter web (no-fixed-optimum check, passed 2026-07-04)

1 rings tension bands → fed on by 3 and AI threat-weighting; 3 is
punished when the fighters settle early and turn on the grower; 4 is
answered by sortie interception (M8) and shields; 5 is answered by
bypass, starvation, tech steps, and blinds taxing slowness; 6 overloads
under simultaneous multi-front pressure.

Balancing note: what keeps a runaway leader in check is **war
arithmetic, not politics** — AI target selection is threat-weighted
(ADR 0025 tendencies), so balancing emerges from threat reading. No
alliance machinery in the MVP; checking the leader is available and
sometimes correct, never mandatory (free-riding is a legitimate line).
