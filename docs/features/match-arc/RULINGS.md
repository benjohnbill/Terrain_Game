## ⑧ Settlement preset ladder (A-1, 2026-07-05, Codex-consulted)

Sealed: three presets as a claim-rate ladder over the composite reach
value — 관대 50% (indemnity-first fill) / 표준 75% (cession-first) /
최대 100%; claim rate = rejection risk, so the ladder slices the
value-vs-certainty-vs-time trade. Rejected alternatives: **2 presets**
(no default button, no tempo-peace seat); **continuous claim slider**
(price-probing minigame against deterministic acceptance);
**composition-only presets** (loses the rejection-risk read). Claim
rates and fill orders were 가안 at sealing, confirmed by sheet 11
(see ⑬). Vassalage is NOT a Maximum replacement (see ⑭).

## ⑨ Shield mass = facing-front reading (A-2, sheet 10, 2026-07-05)

Sealed: shield mass = field army + border-shield garrisons on the
fronts FACING the counting side (late-Roman limitanei/comitatenses
split). Derived per turn from adjacency, zero new state — conquest
inherits exposure, vassalage widens the shared front, a
no-shared-front realm counts at field-only. User rider registered:
expansion break-even card (display debt, handoff §A-4).

## ⑩ Projectable-mass formula (A-2, sheet 10, 2026-07-05)

Sealed: projectable = min(field army, Σ exit-door width × 2), with
flow 2 and floor 1,000 (reuse of the raid visibility threshold).
Flow 2 is the unique value separating the archetypes correctly: pass
realms (촉-type, 1,000×2 = 2,000) stay IN the balance as small-punch
blockers (Parthia pattern); strait realms (hermit-kingdom type,
500×2 = 1,000) sit outside with a working staging buy-back (+500
door → 2,000 = the 임진왜란 move).

## ⑪ Regeneration window W = 6 (A-2, sheet 10, 2026-07-05)

Sealed: W = 6 turns as the time depth of "irreversible" — the
coalition side counts current projection + 6 turns of recruitment
(reuses the M12/M13 recovery anchors); leadership takes no W, present
punch only. Ledger evidence: check trips T22 of a 26-turn arc; a
single war from a parity start falls ~10,000 short — structurally
unreachable. The ~1.7 ratio validated alongside: trip moves ±1 turn
per ±0.2; the binding lever is the shield base, not the ratio. User
rider registered: hegemony work-list card + reachability filter on
coalition sums (display debts, handoff §A-4).

## ⑫ Resistance discount ×0.6 (A-2, sheet 11, 2026-07-05)

Sealed: continued-war expected loss carries a ×0.6 resistance
discount — the loser prices fighting on below the full composite bill
(the winner also bleeds, time flows, third parties move — 강화 결렬
must be possible). This is what keeps the preset ladder
differentiated. Verified: conciliatory-Maximum acceptance 40% =
registered bar passes; no always-right preset; hardliners never sign
Maximum. Fog-design rider (Codex finding, accepted): repeated
observation will reverse-engineer a realm's temperament class, so the
outlook band's tension must be carried by the
expected-continued-war-loss fog, not by coefficient uncertainty —
consistent with the sealed structure (deterministic AI, banded player
read).

## ⑬ Lenient = tempo-peace preset (A-2, sheet 11, 2026-07-05)

Sealed: 관대's product is acceptance CERTAINTY (it buys out refusal
risk) — the natural pick when the acceptance-outlook band is wide,
i.e. a price tag on scouting neglect. Sheet-11 verdicts: claim rates
50/75/100 CONFIRMED; fill-order decoupling verified — rate carries
acceptance risk, composition carries the archetype benefit (cession
feeds conquest-snowball, indemnity feeds raid-attrition).

## ⑭ Vassalage priced in acceptance currency (A-2, 2026-07-05)

Sealed (STRUCTURE): vassalage is priced in acceptance currency, never
reach currency. Reach-currency pricing is structurally broken:
hegemony swing ≈ 2× loser remaining mass per few yield forgone,
~15–20:1 over recruitment → always the buy. Vassal bundle =
standard-preset material + sovereignty premium (주권 프리미엄) ×
loser remaining value. Premium value stayed 가안 gated on sheet 12 —
paid by ⑯. Alignment note: the 복속 row's earlier "loser's side"
phrasing was a drafting error, corrected 2026-07-05 to "overlord's
side" (matches the 정산 통화 row).

## ⑮ Cap growth = the match-closure lever (sheet 12, 2026-07-05)

Sealed (STRUCTURE, owned by MAGNITUDE M13): with static national caps
the hegemony check is near-unreachable at match scale — L2 evidence:
96% of bot matches never trip (leadership cannot clear a healthy
same-size peer while blood falls on pools, not caps). The closure
lever is cap growth through conquest/development. Numbers went to A-3
(M14, capPerPop 600); the owed sheet-12 re-run was paid at candidate
values (27% closure, spread intact) — re-run again if M14 numbers
move.

## ⑯ Sovereignty premium 0.25 (sheet 12, 2026-07-05)

Sealed: premium = 0.25 (pays the ⑭ gate). L2 evidence: 0.15
overpowers the chain (62% of demands accepted; vassal-chain top of
the field); 0.25 sits at the knee (28% accepted, in-family win rate);
0.35 buys nothing more. Rider sealed with it: the premium is a
**floor-setter, not a fine dial** — above 0.25 acceptance saturates
because capital-risk dominates the loser's margin; future
chain-weakening belongs to the loss model's capital-risk side, not
this number. Shape at the sealed discount: kneeling is rare and
decisive-shaped — conciliatory kneels under decisive victory +
capital threat, pragmatic only in the largest cases, hardliners NEVER
(nor do they sign Maximum: vs a hardliner the ceiling is standard
terms or actual conquest — playtest watch: hardliner-majority worlds
lengthen matches).
